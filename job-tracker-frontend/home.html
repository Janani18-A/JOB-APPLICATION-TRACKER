Frequently Asked Questions

What is a job application tracker?

A job application tracker is a smart tool for organizing and monitoring your job search. It keeps all your applications, updates, deadlines, and employer contacts in one easy-to-access place.

How do I use a job tracker?

You can track your job applications, see their status, and get reminders about deadlines to stay organized.

Can I use the job tracker for free?

Yes! You can use the basic features of our job tracker for free.

How can I create an account?

To create an account, go to the Home page and click the Login button. Then, fill in your details to sign up and start using the job application tracker..
